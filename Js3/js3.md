6.	Create a BMI Calculator 
BMI = Weight(kg) / ((height(m) * height(m))

ans:
   let weight = prompt("Enter your weight (kg):");
   let height = prompt("Enter your height (m):");
   let BMI = weight / (height * height);
   alert("BMI = " + BMI);
